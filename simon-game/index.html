1) Click on the title to start
2) Click on the tile that flashes
3) A new tile will flash
4) Click on all the tiles in the sequence in order
5) If you repeat the sequence correctly, the game will add another step to the sequence
6) If you make a mistake, the game will end, and you will have to start over
7) Continue playing and repeating the sequence correctly until you set a highscore, or until you make a mistake
8) As you progress through the levels, the sequences will become longer and more complicated, making it harder to remember and repeat them accurately.
9) Compete with your friends to see who has the highest memory!!
